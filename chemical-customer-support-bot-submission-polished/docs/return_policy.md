# Return and Claims Policy

## Returns
Returns are accepted only with prior authorization. Opened hazardous materials and custom-packed products are generally non-returnable.

## Damaged Shipments
Customers should report visible damage within 48 hours of receipt and provide photos of the packaging and labels.

## Wrong Item Claims
Wrong-item claims should include order reference, product label photo, and unopened package condition where possible.

## Delivery Delays
Transit delays caused by weather, customs, carrier restrictions, or hazmat routing may not qualify for refund, but should be escalated for manual review.
