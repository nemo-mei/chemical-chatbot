# Shipping Policy

## Standard Processing
Standard catalog items usually ship within 2 to 7 business days depending on inventory, packaging format, and destination.

## Hazardous Materials
Products flagged as hazardous may require:
- approved packaging
- carrier availability review
- documentation checks
- destination restrictions review

Hazmat surcharge is added according to the region pricing table.

## Refrigerated or Temperature-Sensitive Items
Some oxidizers and unstable products may require additional packaging controls. The chatbot may include a refrigerated surcharge in preliminary quotes when applicable.

## International Orders
International orders are preliminary until export control, destination documentation, and packaging compatibility are reviewed by staff.

## Rural and Limited-Service Areas
Additional fees may apply for limited-service or rural addresses.
