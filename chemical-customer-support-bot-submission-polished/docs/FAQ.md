# Chemical Support Bot Knowledge Base FAQ

## General Ordering
### What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
MOQ varies by product. Most standard products in the catalog start at 20 kg or 25 kg. Specialty solvents such as DMSO may start at 10 kg.

### Can I request a sample before placing a larger order?
Yes. Sample requests are reviewed case by case. Common screening criteria include item availability, destination, intended use, and shipping restrictions.

### Can I ask for a quote in a different unit?
Yes. The sales team can often convert quotes between kg, L, drum, pail, and IBC formats. Final billing depends on packaging and density assumptions.

## Product Information
### Can the chatbot provide SDS files?
The chatbot can answer common safety and storage questions from the internal knowledge base, but official SDS distribution should still be handled through your company document process.

### Can the chatbot confirm food grade, USP, or pharma grade?
It can provide the standard catalog grade and indicate whether alternate grades may be available. Final confirmation should be reviewed by sales or quality staff.

## Shipping
### Do you ship hazardous materials?
Yes, some products can ship as hazardous materials where legally permitted. Hazmat shipments may add surcharges and require extra compliance checks.

### Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, preliminary quotes can be generated for common international destinations. Final export documents, import rules, and carrier constraints may change the final commercial quote.

## Support Limits
### When should the request be escalated to a human?
Escalation is recommended when the customer requests:
- custom formulation
- regulated-use review
- a missing catalog item
- payment dispute
- delivery complaint
- formal SDS or COA release
- contract pricing
