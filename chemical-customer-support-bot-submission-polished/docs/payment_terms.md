# Payment Terms

## Standard Terms
Default terms for new customers are prepaid or credit card before shipment.

## Approved Accounts
Net 30 terms may be available for approved business customers after account review.

## Quote Validity
Preliminary chatbot quotes are typically valid for 7 calendar days unless raw-material volatility or freight conditions change.

## Taxes and Duties
Domestic taxes and international duties are not always included in the preliminary quote unless specifically stated.
