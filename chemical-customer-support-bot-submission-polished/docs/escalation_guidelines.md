# Escalation Guidelines

Escalate the conversation when any of the following appear:
- customer asks for a product not present in the catalog
- customer needs a formal commercial invoice or contract pricing
- customer requests technical suitability confirmation for a regulated application
- customer asks for legal, environmental, or medical advice
- customer disputes charges, lead time, or shipment damage
- customer asks for an official SDS, COA, or batch-specific document
- the bot cannot confidently identify the requested chemical or destination

When escalating, log:
- timestamp
- customer message
- probable intent
- missing information
- suggested human owner (sales, logistics, quality, technical service)
